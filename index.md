## ❓ Common questions

**Does this software store my data online?**
No. The software keeps your data on devices you control. You choose the location for your backup files.

**How often should I back up my computer?**
Schedule backups once a week if you add new files often. Run a manual backup before you install any major system updates or new software.

**Can I view the files inside my backup?**
Yes. Use the mount feature to assign a drive letter to your backup. This allows you to explore the image, copy individual files out, and paste them to your computer.

**Is my data safe during the backup process?**
The software performs a read-only operation on your primary drive. It does not change your existing files while it creates the backup image. 

**What if my computer cannot boot?**
The software provides tools to create rescue media. Use a USB flash drive to create a bootable rescue tool. If your computer fails to start, plug in the USB drive and boot from it to restore your system image.
